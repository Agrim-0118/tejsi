Landslide

Landslides are rapid downslope movements of rock and soil triggered by rain, earthquakes, or human activity.

About

Landslides destroy infrastructure and lives. Deforestation and mining increase risks. Prevention includes slope stabilization, early warning systems, and careful land use planning.
